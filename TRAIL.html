M.Tech(Master of Technology)

ABOUT

Manipal Institute of Technology offers MTech courses in various engineering streams, ranging from classic engineering streams such as mechanical, civil, and production engineering to high-tech streams like automobile and aeronautical. The duration of the MTech program is 2 years, which is divided into 4 semesters. There are 15 streams of study available at the MTech level with Manipal Institute of Technology:

  1. Chemical
    1. Industrial Biotechnology
    2. Chemical engineering
  3. Civil
  4. Computer
    1. Computer science & engg
    2. Software engg
    3. Machine learning &Decision tree
  5. Electrical
    1. Aerospace Engg
    2. Biomedical Engg
    3. Control Systems
    4. Digital Electronics & Communication Engg
  6. Management
  7. Mechanical
    1. Automobile engg
    2. Defence Technology
    3. Manufacturing engg
    4. Thermal Science &energy systems


Scope of M. Tech Degree from Manipal Institute of Technology:
An MTech degree from one of the leading universities in India will leverage students to fulfil their career goals and find exciting employment opportunities. They will be equipped with the essential skills and knowledge that will enable them to succeed in their profession.



ELIGIBILITY>

MAHE Manipal admits candidates under General or Foreign / NRI category, on fulfilling the eligibility requirements and following the respective admission processes. All admissions are on the basis of merit. Foreign / NRI category seats are only available for foreign nationals / candidates with NRI status or those sponsored by relatives with NRI status. Foreign / NRI category seats are NOT equivalent to payment / management / reserved seats
